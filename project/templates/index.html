OUR GOAL

"New to sports? Want to learn the right techniques and do effective exercises? PTWA can help you reach your goals!" Exercising is an important part of a healthy lifestyle. However, using the correct techniques and minimizing injury risk while exercising is crucial. With PTWA, we aim to ensure that everyone exercises correctly, safely, and for free. Thanks to our advanced image processing technology, we analyze users' exercise movements in real-time and correct mistakes. This way, users get the most out of each exercise.

Strength and Endurance

Exercising increases your strength and endurance. Regular exercises strengthen your body and help you stay active for longer periods.

Fitness and Flexibility

Exercises not only improve fitness but also increase flexibility. Flexibility helps you move more comfortably and effectively in daily activities.

Mental Health and Well-being

Exercising benefits not only your physical health but also your mental health. Conscious exercises reduce stress and help you feel happier and more focused.
